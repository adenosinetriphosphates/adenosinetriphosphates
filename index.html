About me

Student, scientist,
builder.

I'm Arnav, a student at Del Norte High School with a deep curiosity for how the world works at a molecular level. From reactions in a test tube to algorithms on a circuit board, I love finding the logic behind complex systems.

Science Olympiad has been a huge part of my journey — competing, collaborating, and pushing myself to learn subjects well beyond the classroom. I've taken home multiple medals across events and love the challenge of preparing for something entirely new each season.

Outside of scioly, I build and program robots, blending engineering with problem-solving. Most recently, my team placed Champions at the FLL Qualifier Rounds — a moment I'm really proud of.
